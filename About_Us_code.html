Why We Do What We Do

For the past 7 years years, The Children's Art Alliance has been working toward music for every child regardless of ability in Chicago. Too many kids today are horrible human beings, because they don't have music.

Research shows that children without exposure to music are less well-round and more likely to use and sell illegal drugs. Through Children's Arts Alliance, there is hope! With the partnership of donors, local musicians and volunteers like yourself, more children will have access to a quality music education and a life without drugs.
